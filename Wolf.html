Wolf’s Gravestone Overview

Wolf’s Gravestone is a 5 star Claymore obtained from the Wish in Genshin Impact.


Wolfish Tracker

Increases ATK by 20/25/30/35/40%. On hit, attacks against opponents with less than 30% HP increase all party members’ ATK .


Wolf’s Gravestone can be used by both DPS and Support role’s character.
