Genshin Impact Elemental Reactions & Resonance



Elemental Debuffs


Each element in Genshin Impact has a different negative effect on your enemies.


Name Element Effect
Condensed Ice Increases stamina consumption.
Slowing Water Increases skill CD durations.
Smoldering Flames Continuously inflicts damage over time.
Engulfing Storm Continuously drains Energy Recharge.

Elemental Reactions


Combining different elements in combat is the key to success in Genshin Impact.


Name Element Effect
Crystalize + Creates a crystal that will provide a shield when picked up.
Swirl + Deals extra elemental damage and spreads the effect.
Electro-Charged + Deals Electro DMG over time.
Overloaded + Deals AoE Pyro DMG.
Frozen + Freezes the target.
Melt + Deals extra damage.
Superconduct + Deals AoE Cryo DMG and reduces the target's Physical RES by 50%.
Vaporize + Deals extra damage.
Burning + Deals AoE Pyro DoT and applies Pyro.
Bloom + Creates a Dendro Core that explodes after 6 seconds, dealing AoE Dendro DMG.
Hyperbloom + Triggered when applying Electro to a Dendro Core. Transforms the Dendro Core into a homing Sprawling Shot that deals increased AoE Dendro DMG in a much smaller AoE.
Burgeon + Triggered when applying Pyro to a Dendro Core. Detonates the Dendro Core immediately, dealing increased AoE Dendro DMG.
Quicken + Applies a Quicken aura.
Aggravate + Triggered when applying Electro to an entity with the Quicken aura. Adds Flat DMG Bonus to the attack that triggers this reaction.
Spread + Triggered when applying Dendro to an entity with the Quicken aura. Adds Flat DMG Bonus to the attack that triggers this reaction.

Elemental Resonance


When you have a full party, you will receive effects that correspond to the Elemental Types present in the party.


Name Requirement Effect
Enduring Rock Increases shield strength by 15%. Additionally, characters protected by a shield will have the following special characteristics: DMG dealt increased by 15%, dealing DMG to enemies will decrease their Geo RES by 20% for 15s.
Fervent Flames Affected by Cryo for 40% less time. Increases ATK by 25%.
Soothing Waters Affected by Pyro for 40% less time. Increases incoming healing by 30%.
Impetuous Winds Decreases Stamina Consumption by 15%. Increases Movement SPD by 10%. Shortens Skill CD by 5%.
High Voltage Affected by Hydro for 40% less time. Superconduct, Overloaded, and Electro-Charged have a 100% chance to generate an Electro Elemental Particle (CD: 5s).
Shattering Ice Affected by Electro for 40% less time. Increases CRIT Rate against enemies that are Frozen or affected by Cryo by 15%.
Sprawling Greenery After triggering Burning, Quicken, or Bloom reactions, all nearby party members gain 30 Elemental Mastery for 6s. After triggering Aggravate, Spread, Hyperbloom, or Burgeon reactions, all nearby party members gain 20 Elemental Mastery for 6s. The durations of the aforementioned effects will be counted independently.
Protective Canopy Any 4 Unique Elements All Elemental RES +15%, Physical RES +15%.
