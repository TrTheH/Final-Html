Thundering Pulse Overview

Thundering Pulse is a 5 star Bow obtained from the Weapon Event Wish in Genshin Impact. It mostly appears along with Yoimiya’s banner.


Rule By Thunder

Increases ATK by 20/25/30/35/40% and grants the might of the Thunder Emblem. At stack levels 1/2/3, the Thunder Emblem increases Normal Attack DMG by 12/15/18/21/24% /// 24/30/36/42/48% // 40/50/60/70/80%. The character will obtain 1 stack of Thunder Emblem in each of the following scenarios: Normal Attack deals DMG (stack lasts 5s), casting Elemental Skill (stack lasts 10s); Energy is less than 100% (stack disappears when Energy is full). Each stack’s duration is calculated independently.
